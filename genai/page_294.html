Description

The Bose Soundbar 900 is a powerful, 7.1-channel soundbar system that brings home theater quality to your living room. With a total of 12 speakers, it includes an internal subwoofer and rear surround drivers for a truly immersive experience. The wireless subwoofer included provides additional bass and depth to the sound.

The Soundbar 900 is also HDMI-ARC compatible, making it easy to connect to your TV. It's a great addition to any home theater system or can be used as a standalone speaker for gaming and music.
