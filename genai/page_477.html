Product Description

The Steam Deck is a handheld gaming PC developed by Valve Corporation. It features a 7-inch touchscreen display, dual Joy-Con controllers, and a battery life of up to 6 hours.

  • Processor: AMD Zen 2
  • RAM: 8GB/16GB
  • Storage: 64GB/256GB SSD
  • Battery Life: Up to 6 hours
  • Display: 7-inch Touchscreen
